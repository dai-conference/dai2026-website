DAI 2026 Call for Contributions Is Open

The working CFP for the 8th International Conference on Distributed AI is now available, covering four submission tracks and seven topic areas: from agent engineering and infrastructure to embodied multi-agent systems and AI for science. Submission instructions are posted on the Authors page.

Submission and Notification Dates Published

Workshop proposals are due 30 June. Abstracts are due 27 July; Research and Industry submissions are due 3 August; AI Paper Track submissions are due 10 August. Notifications go out 16 September; camera-ready is due 16 October.
