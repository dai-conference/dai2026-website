Welcome

DAI 2026 invites the international distributed AI community to Hong Kong for four days of research exchange on the science, systems, and societies of agentic AI. The program centres on four submission tracks and seven topic areas spanning agent infrastructure, learning, coordination, markets, embodied systems, and AI for science.

Calls

Calls for Contributions

DAI 2026 welcomes original research in distributed and agentic AI, studies of AI agents as substantive participants in scientific discovery, industry papers on deployed systems, and non-archival presentations of relevant work accepted at sister conferences. The conference also invites workshop proposals and tutorials for the broader DAI community.

Research Track

Original research on the theory, methods, systems, evaluation, and applications of distributed AI, autonomous agents, multi-agent systems, and agentic AI.

8 pages double blind 3 Aug

Industry Track

Experience papers, case studies, system descriptions, benchmark reports, and demo proposals from teams deploying distributed and agentic AI in real settings.

6 pages deployment single blind

AI Paper Track

An experimental venue for studying AI agents as participants in research, including fully autonomous and human-AI collaborative scientific workflows.

autonomy disclosure awards 10 Aug

Sister Conference Track

Non-archival presentations of relevant work already accepted by AAMAS, AAAI, IJCAI, EC, KDD, ICLR, ICML, NeurIPS, and related venues.
