Sponsorship & support

DAI 2026 sponsorship opportunities will be announced as the program and industry roles are finalised. Supporters help make student participation, workshops, tutorials, and community events possible.

Tiers · 01

Partnership categories

The final prospectus will include contribution levels, benefits, logo placement, exhibit options, and opportunities for earmarked support. The categories below preview the intended structure.

Community Partner TBA/ prospectus
  • Logo on conference website and program
  • Acknowledgment in public sponsor materials
  • Support for community sessions and student participation
  • Optional recruiting or outreach visibility
Research Program Partner TBA/ prospectus
  • Everything in Community Partner
  • Named support for a workshop, tutorial, poster session, or award
  • Visibility in program and venue signage
  • Exhibit or demo presence subject to venue capacity
Strategic Partner TBA/ prospectus
  • Everything in Research Program Partner
  • Premier visibility across opening and closing materials
  • Named support for high-impact conference activities
  • Bespoke collaboration with the organizing committee

Targeted support · 02

Earmarked sponsorships

Sponsors may earmark contributions toward specific programs that have outsized community impact.

Travel grants

Student travel fund

Subsidises attendance for students and early-career researchers from under-represented regions. ≈ HK$ 8k per grant.

DEI

Diversity scholarships

Targeted scholarships supporting attendance from groups historically under-represented in distributed AI research.

Awards

Best paper / track awards

Sponsor a named best-paper or track-level award, presented in the closing session.

Community

Workshop & tutorial sponsorship

Underwrite a specific workshop or tutorial to keep registration free for community participants.
