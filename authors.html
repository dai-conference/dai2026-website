Open Calls

DAI 2026 invites contributions across research papers, industry papers, AI Paper Track submissions, sister-conference presentations, workshops, and tutorials. The pages below collect the relevant calls and track-specific guidance.

Call for Papers - Research Track

Original research on the theory, methods, systems, evaluation, and applications of distributed AI, autonomous agents, multi-agent systems, and agentic AI.

8 pages double blind 3 Aug

Call for Papers - Industry Track

Industry papers on deployed systems, case studies, benchmarks, demos, and real-world agentic AI experience.

6 pages deployment 3 Aug

Call for Papers - AI Paper Track

An experimental venue for studying AI agents as participants in research, including autonomous and human-AI collaborative scientific workflows.

autonomy disclosure awards 10 Aug

Call for Papers - Sister Conference Track

Non-archival presentations of relevant work already accepted by AAMAS, AAAI, IJCAI, EC, KDD, ICLR, ICML, NeurIPS, and related venues.

rolling opens 1 Jul non archival
